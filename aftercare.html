Tattoo Aftercare

Adhesive Film

  • Keep first bandage on 3–24 hours depending on fluid.
  • Wash with mild fragrance-free soap.
  • Air dry or pat dry with clean towel.
  • Apply second bandage for 3–6 days if needed.
  • Avoid soaking, swimming, or heavy sweating.

Wet Heal

  • Leave initial bandage 3–5 hours.
  • Wash with lukewarm water + antibacterial soap.
  • Apply very thin layer of aftercare balm.
  • Repeat several times daily for first few days.
  • Avoid long showers, pools, hot tubs, or ocean.
  • Do not pick or scratch peeling skin.

Dry Heal

  • Wash gently with antibacterial soap.
  • Pat dry — never rub.
  • Light layer of balm 2–3 times daily.
  • Avoid soaking or prolonged water exposure.
  • Do not pick or scratch during healing.
